Our Story, Our Values

Committed to Excellence in Disability Support

Our Journey

Over a Decade of Transforming Lives

Founded in 2013, Care by nurse began with a simple mission: to deliver compassionate, person-centred support that truly makes a difference. What started as a small team of dedicated support workers has grown into a comprehensive service provider across multiple regions.

Our growth has always been guided by participant feedback and the evolving needs of the disability community. We're proud to have supported over 1,200 participants in achieving their goals and living more independently.

Our Core Values Define Everything We Do

Person-Centred Approach

Every service we provide begins with listening to and understanding the individual. Your goals, preferences and needs guide every decision we make together.

Empowerment

We focus on building skills, confidence and independence. Our supports are designed to help participants take greater control of their lives.

Inclusion

We actively work to break down barriers and create opportunities for full community participation. Diversity is celebrated in our team and among our participants.

Integrity

We maintain the highest ethical standards, with transparent communication and honest advice about what's truly in your best interest.

Innovation

We continuously improve our services by embracing evidence-based practices and creative solutions tailored to individual needs.

Collaboration

We work in partnership with participants, families, and other providers to deliver coordinated support that achieves meaningful outcomes.
