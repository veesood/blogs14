With over 10 years of dedicated disability support experience, our team combines compassionate care with industry expertise. We're committed to person-centred approaches that respect your choices and goals.

Our Services

Comprehensive services designed to help you live independently and achieve your personal goals

Nursing Support

Nursing support providing personalized care. We offer expert assistance with medication, recovery, and daily health management, ensuring comfort and independence for you at home..

Our nursing support focuses on helping you manage complex health tasks, from medication to wound care, allowing you to maintain your independence and well-being..

Supported Independent Living

24/7 home and living supports that promote autonomy in shared or individual accommodation. Our SIL services help you develop daily living skills while receiving the right level of support.

We match you with compatible housemates and support workers who respect your routines and preferences.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a nurse or care service?

You can book through our website, call our care line, or request a free care consultation to discuss your needs.

Yes, we provide flexible scheduling, including overnight and round-the-clock care if needed..

How do you ensure your services are person-centred?

Person-centred care is at the heart of everything we do. We begin by understanding your unique goals, preferences and needs through comprehensive assessments and conversations. Our support plans are co-designed with you and regularly reviewed.

You choose your support workers, set your schedule, and decide how services are delivered. We use communication tools tailored to your needs and always seek your feedback to improve our services.

What areas do you service?

We currently provide services across Melbourne and Canberra. Our team operates in both metropolitan and regional locations, with some services available remotely via telehealth.

If you're outside our standard service area, we may still be able to assist through our network of partner providers - contact us to discuss your specific location needs.

How quickly can services start after initial contact?

We aim to begin services within 2 weeks of initial contact for most supports. For urgent cases, we can often arrange temporary supports within 48 hours while we complete the full assessment process.

The exact timeline depends on your specific needs, current plan status, and any required assessments. Our team will provide a clear timeline during your initial consultation.

What if I need to change or cancel services?

You can change or cancel services at any time - we require just 48 hours notice for most changes. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees.

We understand that needs change, and we'll work with you to adjust your supports accordingly. Our priority is ensuring you have the right services at the right time.
